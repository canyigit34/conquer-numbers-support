Support

Need help with Conquer Numbers: Merge War? Email yigitist94@gmail.com. Please include your device model, iOS version, and a short description of the issue.

How do I deploy a unit?

Merge matching orbs until an orb reaches 8 or higher. Then drag it to the battlefield or double-tap it.

What do the colors mean?

  • Red: melee units
  • Blue: tank units
  • Green: ranged archer units

Where is my progress saved?

Progress is saved locally on your device. No account is required, and the game can be played offline. Deleting the app may remove saved progress.
